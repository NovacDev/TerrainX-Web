TerrainX

NoVac Website

A fork of Terrain3D

Modified by NoVac Games specially designed for large and long-term projects, strong performance and stability.

Features

  • Written in C++, with mesh processing done by the GPU for the highest performance
  • Terrains ranging from 64x64m up to 65.5x65.5km (4295 km²) in variable sized regions
  • Up to 32 texture sets using albedo, normal, roughness, and height
  • Up to 10 Levels of Detail (LODs)
  • Geometric Clipmap Mesh Terrain, as used in The Witcher 3
  • Foliage instancing
  • Imports heightmaps from 3rd party tools
  • Designed as a GDExtension plugin, compatible with official Godot Engine builds
  • Accessible from GDScript, C#, and any language supported by Godot
  • Fully Open Source

Realistic Environments

TerrainX lets you create complex and realistic environments for your games. With support for high-resolution textures, multiple levels of detail, and a powerful vegetation instantiation system, you'll be able to generate vast worlds without compromising performance.

Advanced Terrain Editing

Shape and sculpt your terrains in real time with powerful, precise tools — perfect for projects of any size.

Seamless World Streaming

Automatically stream only the needed parts of the world, ensuring top performance even in massive open-world environments.

Dynamic Weather and Seasons

Easily integrate dynamic weather effects and seasonal changes to bring your worlds to life with realism and variety.
